Barangay Projects

Improvement of Day Care Center

Barangay Pansol's Daycare Center improvement and maintenance as a yearly project of barangay means that the local government of Barangay Pansol has allocated resources and funding to continuously improve and maintain the facilities, equipment, and services provided by the daycare center to its constituents. By making this a yearly project, the barangay government ensures that the daycare center remains in good condition and meets the changing needs of the community. This includes regular repairs, renovations, and upgrades to the building and equipment, as well as the hiring and training of qualified daycare staff.

Construction of Level II Water Supply System

The implementation of the second level water supply system commenced two weeks ago and is currently ongoing. The objective is to provide water to Barangay Pansol Lopez, Quezon at a total cost of Php 3,992,140.05, which will be funded by LGSF-SBDP 2022. YOLOBUILDERS, CO. has been contracted as the supplier.
