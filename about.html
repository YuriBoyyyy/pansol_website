About Pansol

History

Long time ago, there were many springs surrounding the town of Lopez that were used by all the people in the area, one time, a drought or a shortage of water came. Almost all the springs from the surrounding area have dried up, but there is one particular spring in the area that continues to produce clean and cold water. The people were very grateful and blessed because of this, the Spaniards called the name Pansol, because in their language, "PANSOL" means "SPRING" since then the place was called Pansol. As of now pansol is surrounded by rice fields and coconuts and has a sea area or what is commonly called a fishpond.

This is the main source of the people of the Barangay to establish their lifestyle while others build a small store to sell their produce such as fish, coconut, rice and it also reaches various stores that are part of the town of Lopez, Pansol is a Parangay in the municipality of Lopez, in the province of Quezon. The population of Pansol grew from 1,440 in 1990 to 2,249 in 2020, an increase of 809 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a negative growth rate of 0.62%, or a decrease of 67 people, from the previous population of 2,316 in 2015.
